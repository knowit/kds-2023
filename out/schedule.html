Call for Presentations

Knowit Developer Summit (KDS) is Knowit's annual internal software developer conference. The 2021 conference will be held on 12 - 13 November at the Scandic Continental in Stockholm, Sweden.

We are now looking for speakers for the conference, the basic idea is to share knowledge and experiences with other developers within Knowit. The length of a presentation can be 10, 30, 60 or 90 minutes. You decide!

For more information about the conference, see the practical information page.

Submission deadline is extended to the 1st of September 2021. You may add just a brief abstract and complete it later; the most important thing right now is to get all the presentations registered. You may submit more than one presentation if you wish. You can also hold a joint presentation; maybe you and a colleague are working on something interesting and would like to present it together?

If you have any questions feel free to contact us at kds@knowit.no
