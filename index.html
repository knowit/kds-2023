Call for presentations

We are now looking for speakers for the conference, the basic idea is to share knowledge and experiences with other developers within Knowit. The length of a presentation can be 10, 30, 60 or 90 minutes. You decide!If you have any questions feel free to contact us at kds@knowit.noThe subject for a talk can vary widely, you can check out last years program here for inspiration.
